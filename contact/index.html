EMP Board

 

Vanessa P. Scott, MD
Executive Director & Founder
Vanessa@ecomedproject.org		 Originally from San Diego, CA, Vanessa attended Harvard University as an undergraduate and Columbia University for the post-bacc premedical program. She graduated from Albert Einstein College of Medicine in May 2013, completed a Pediatric Residency at UCSD/Rady Children’s Hospital in San Diego, CA and Mount Sinai in New York, NY. She currently is a general pediatrician at New York Presbyterian Hospital, a Post-doctoral Fellow in Community Health (Academic Pediatrics) at Columbia University, and a candidate for a MSc in Epidemiology at Mailman Public School of Health. Founding and running the EMP has been truly inspiring and she looks forward to expanding the program in the future.
