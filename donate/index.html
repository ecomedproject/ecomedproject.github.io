100% of donations go towards establishing programs at the Sankande Medical Clinic and the Sioma district Hospital, as well as the thousands of patients who depend on these institutions. The Sankande Clinic was built solely on donations like yours and now serves as a delivery room, ante and post natal care ward, a mother and child care center, general outpatient clinic, and an emergency outpost for the Sioma Medical Clinic. Sankande is a highly populated area with the primary and secondary schools close by. Before, patients had to walk 3-4 hours to the Sioma Clinic if in labor, needed a check up, or in an emergency. The Sankande Outpost has helped patients who need medical attention faster.

The EMP Board is comprised of volunteers only and they do not receive any portion of the donation.
